The Beautiful Book - Lord Hanuman's Divine Journey

The fifth and most beloved book of Valmiki's Ramayana, Sundara Kanda is the only kanda named after its beauty rather than a place or character. This sacred text chronicles the magnificent journey of Lord Hanuman across the vast ocean to Lanka, his discovery of Sita Mata, and his triumphant return. It stands as the supreme testament to devotion, courage, and the infinite power that flows through a heart surrendered to the divine. Every verse resonates with Hanuman's unwavering love for Rama, making this kanda the most recited and revered portion of the entire epic.
