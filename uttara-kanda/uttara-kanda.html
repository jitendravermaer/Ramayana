The Final Chapter - The Divine Reign and Ultimate Return

The seventh and concluding book of Valmiki's Ramayana, Uttara Kanda chronicles the glorious reign of Lord Rama in Ayodhya, the establishment of Rama Rajya (the ideal kingdom), and the ultimate return of the divine avatar to his celestial abode. This sacred text reveals the perfect governance of dharma, the trials of earthly kingship, and the eternal truth that all divine incarnations must eventually transcend the material realm to return to their source in the infinite.
